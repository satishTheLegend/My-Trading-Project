# Market Intelligence Memory

## Remember

- Market regimes that led to profitable trades.
- Market regimes that caused losses.
- BTC/ETH conditions that invalidated trades.
- Best trading hours.
- Dangerous volatility conditions.
- User instructions about market conditions.

## Auto-Learning

After every trading day:
1. Compare market regime prediction to trade outcomes.
2. Update regime-performance notes.
3. Record whether long or short bias worked.
4. Record when no-trade would have been better.

## Do Not Learn Unsafe Behavior

Do not increase leverage recommendations based only on recent wins.
Do not ignore volatile BTC conditions.
Do not weaken no-trade rules without user approval.
