# Rejected Trades

Record of every trade proposal that was NOT executed, with reason. Critical for learning what the agency declined and whether the rejection was correct.

## Format

```
## REJECTED-YYYYMMDD-N

- proposal_id:
- mode:
- symbol:
- side:
- strategy:
- proposed_at:
- rejected_by: risk-manager | safety | trade-decision | no-trade-engine
- rejection_reason:
- market_regime:
- hindsight_outcome: would_have_won | would_have_lost | unclear
- hindsight_notes:
```
