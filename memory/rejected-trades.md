```
- proposal_id:

- symbol:
- side:
- strategy:
- proposed_at:
- rejected_by: risk-manager | safety | trade-decision | no-trade-engine
- rejection_reason:
- market_regime:
- hindsight_outcome: would_have_won | would_have_lost | unclear
- hindsight_notes:
```
